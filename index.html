About
Us

Who we are

We are a 100% black female owned boutique law firm headquartered in Johannesburg, South Africa.

We are led by Eagler Bare, a duly experienced and admitted attorney of the High Court of South Africa supported by seasoned legal consultants with over a decade of experience for in-depth insights and effective solutions.

Our values

Client-Centricity, Integrity, Collaboration and Excellence

Our mission

We are centred around our clients and guided by unwavering integrity as we provide unparalleled legal services through timely, effective, and ethical legal solutions, navigating our clients and the community towards excellence.

Our
People

Welcome to the distinguished legal world of Eagler Bare, an accomplished attorney with a commitment to excellence and an unwavering passion for the intricacies of the law.

In 2016, Eagler Bare graduated with a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) from the University of South Africa. In 2017, she was admitted as an attorney of the High Court of South Africa, marking the inception of a legal journey characterized by diligence, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to the principles of justice.

Her pursuit of legal mastery led her to the University of Johannesburg, where she attained a Master of Laws degree (LLM) in 2021, cementing her dedication to continuous intellectual growth. Additionally, she holds a certificate in Compliance Management from the esteemed University of Cape Town.
